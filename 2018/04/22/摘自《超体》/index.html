摘自电影《超体》

  • Life was given to us a billion years ago, what have we done with it?

    十亿年前上天赋予我们生命，演化至今的结果是什么？

  • If life thrived approximately a billion years ago, we’ll have to wait 400,000 years to see the end of race of the first nerve cells. This is where life as we know it begins. Brain’s formation of only a few miligrams, is not possible to determine any sign of intelligence yet.
    It acts more as a reflect.One neuron, you’re alive. Two neurons, you’re moving. And with movement, interesting things began to happen.
    Animal life on Earth goes back millions of years. Yet, most specie only use 3~5% of its celebral capacity. But it’s not until we reached human being, at the top of the animal chain, that we finally see a specie used more of its celebral capacity. 10% may not seem like much but it’s a lot if we look at all we’ve done with it.

    生命出现在大约几十亿年前，但还要再过四十万年，第一批神经细胞才会发生畸变。这就是我们所知的生命起源。大脑的重量只有几毫克，完全不可能有任何智力的表现，反而比较像是反射作用。一个神经元让你生存，两个神经元，让你移动。可以行动后，就会发生有趣的事。
    地球上的生物可以追溯到几百万年前，但大多数生物只有运用3%~5%的脑力。直到人类出现，成为动物之王，终于有物种可以使用更多脑力。10%看似不多，但只要回顾人类用这10%做过什么，就很可观了。

  • Now let’s discuss a special case. The only living being that uses it’s brain better than us, the dolphin. It’s estimated that this incredible animal, uses up to 20% of its celebral capacity. And in particular, this allows it to have an echo-location system that is more efficient than any sonar invented by mankind. But the dolphin did not invent the sonar, it developed it naturally. And this is the crucial part of our philosophical reflection we’ve today. Can we therefore conclude that humans are concerned more with “having” than “being”.

    现在让我们来探讨一个特殊案例。唯一运用大脑比我们更厉害的生物，就是海豚。这种不可思议的动物据估计，运用高达20%的脑力。尤其是，这让它们拥有回声定位系统，比人类发明的声纳装置更有效率。但海豚并没有发明声纳，而是自然演化出声纳。这也是我们目前最重要的哲学思辨范畴。我们是否可以说，人类更关注的是“拥有”而不是“存有”。

  • For primitive beings like us, life seems to only have one single purpose. Gaming time. And its going through time, seems to be also the only real purpose of each of the cell in our bodies. To ahieve that aim, the mass of the cell that make up earth worm and human beings has only 2 solutions. Be a mortal, or to reproduce. If its habitat is not sufficiently favorable or nurturing, the cell will choose immortality. In another word, self-sufficiency and self-management. On the other hand, if the habitat is favorable, they will choose to reproduce. That way, when they die, they hand down essential information and knowledge to the next cell, which hands it down to the next cell and so on. Thus, knowledge and learning all handed down through time.

    像我们这种原始生物，活着似乎只有一个目的：争取时间。时间的不足又让永恒延续成为我们体内每个细胞的唯一目标。为达到这个目标，那些构筑小至蚯蚓大至人类的细胞只有两个选择：永生或繁衍。如果栖息地不好，不适合生长，细胞就会选择永生，也就是自足和自理。另一方面，如果栖息地足够良好，细胞就会选择繁衍。这样死后就可以把重要的咨询和知识交给下一代细胞，无限传下去。因此知识和学习，都会随着时间，不断传承。

  • Let’s imagine for a few moments, what our life would be like if we could access, let’s say, 20% of our brains’ capacity? This first stage would give us access to and control of our own body.For the moment, it’s just hypothesis, I confess. But if you think about it, it’s troubling to realize that the Greeks, the Egyptians and the Indians had notion of “cells” centuries before the invention of the microscope and what to say about Darwin whom everybody took for a fool when he put forward his Theory of Evolution. It’s up to us to push the rules and laws and go from “evolution” to “revolution”. 100 billion neurons per human, of which only 15% are activated. There are more connections in the human body than there’re stars in the galaxy. We possess a gigantic network of information to which we’ve almost no access.

    让我们先想象一下，假如我们可以运用20%的脑力，生活会变成什么样？在这第一阶段，我们可以尽情发挥并控制自己的身体。老实说，目前只是假设。但不妨想想看，我们很难理解为什么许多古文明早在显微镜发明以前，就知道细胞的存在。还有达尔文，他提出演化论被当成傻瓜，要不要挑战既有的法则，进化再进化，取决于我们自己。每个人拥有一千亿个神经元，我们只启动15%。人体内部的神经连结多过天上繁星，我们拥有庞大的资讯网络，却几乎没有在使用。

    The next stage would probably be control of other people. But for that, we will need to access at least 40% of our brains’ capacity. And after control of ourselves and others, come control of matter. But now we’re entering the realm of science fiction. And we don’t know anything more than a dog who watches the moon.

    下一阶段可能是控制他人，但要做到这样，我们必须至少运用40%的脑力。控制自己和他人后，就是控制物质。但这个就是科幻小说的内容了，我们就像小狗观月，无法理解。

  • I feel everthing. Space, the air, the vibrations, the people. I can feel the gravity. I can feel the rotation of the Earth. The heat Leaving my body, the blood in my veins. I can my brain. The deepest parts of my memory.

    我能感觉到一切。空间，大气，地球的律动，所有人。我能感受到重力，我可以觉察到地球自转，我身体散发的热能，我血管里的血液。我可以感受到我的大脑，我记忆的最深曾。

  • Learning is always a painful process. Like when you’re little, your bones are growing and you ached all over. Can you believe I can remember the sound of my own bones growing? Like this “grinding” under the skin. Everything is different now. Like…sound of music that I can understand, like fluids. It’s funny I used to be so concern with who I was and what I wanted to be, and now I’ve access to the furthest reaches of my brain. I see things clearly and realize what make us “us”,it’s primitive. They are all obstacles. Does that make any sense? Like this pain you’re experiencing is blocking you from understanding. All you know now is pain. That’s all you know,pain.

    学习是痛苦的过程，就像小时候骨骼正在生长，全身酸痛。我还记得骨骼生长的声音，你信么？就像这样在皮肤底下嘎嘎作响。我对所有的感观都变了，各种声音变成我所欣赏的音乐，不停地流动。有趣的是我以前一直在想我是谁、我想成为什么人，但现在我可以探索大脑最深处，事情变得更透彻并且体会到我们人类一切感官，太过原始，反倒成了阻碍。懂么？像你现在经历的疼痛，就在妨碍你理解。你现在只知道痛，除了痛还是痛。

  • I don’t feel pain,fear,desire. It’s like all things that make us human are fading away. It’s like the less human I feel, all these knowledge about everything. Quantum Physics, Applied Mathematics, the infinite capacity of the cell’s nucleus, they’re all exploding inside my brain, all these knowledge. I don’t know what to do with it.

    我感受不到疼痛、恐惧、欲望。我似乎正在一点一点消失人性。我愈不像人类，愈能够领悟宇宙万物的知识，量子物理、应用数学、细胞核的无限潜能。新知识不断出现在我脑内，我不知道该做什。

    If you’re asking me what to do…You know, you think about it, the very nature of life. I mean, from the very begining. The development of the first cell and divided into two cells. This whole purpose of life has been, passed on what was learned. There is no higher purpose. So if you’re asking me what to do with all these konwledge you’re accumalating, I’d say…pass it on, just like any simple cell, going through time.

    如果你问我我的意见……这样吧，你想想，生命的本质，我是说最早的时候，第一个细胞的出现，然后分裂成两个。生命只有一个目的，那就是分享及传递所学的东西。没有什么比这个更重要了。如果问我怎么处置你不断累积的知识，我会说分享给别人，就像任何细胞，随着时间老化。

  • To knowledge.

  • We never really die.

  • Q: How did you manage to access all these information?
    A: Electrical impulses.
    Every cell knows and talks to every other cell. They exchange a thousand bits of information between them per second. Cell group together, forming a giant web of information which in turn forms matter. Cells get together, take on one form, deform, reform, makes no difference, it’s all the same.

    每个细胞都懂的互相沟通，每秒可以交换一千位元的信息。细胞聚在一起，形成巨大的沟通网络，然后构成物质。细胞结合以后，变成另一种形式，变形，在成形，没什么特别的，宇宙万物都一样。

    Human considers themselves unique. So they rooted their whole theory of existence on their uniqueness. “One” is the unit of measure, but it’s not. All social systems we put into place are mere ‘sketch’. ‘1+1=2’That’s all we learned. But one plus one has never equalled two. They are in fact, no numbers and no letters. We codify our existence to bring it down to human size, to make it comprehensible. We created the scale so that we can forget its unfathomable scale.

    人类总以为自己是独特的，存在理论总是以独特性为基础。“1”是度量单位，但它不是。至今所有社会体系，都只是初步的蓝图。“1+1=2”我们都是这样学的。但1+1从来不等于2。这世界上根本没有数字，也没有字母。我们把自己的存在加以简化成“人类”，以方便理解。我们创造出度量衡，好忘却这一切都是深不可测。

    But if humans are not the unit of measure, and the world isn’t governed by mathemathical laws, what governs all that?

    但假如人类不是一个“单位”，世界也不是靠数学法则来主宰，那什么才是？

    Form a car speeding down the road, speed up the image infinitely, and the car disappears. So, what proof do we have of its existence? Time gives legitimacy to its existence. Time is the only true unit fo measure. It gives proof to the existence of matter. Without time, we don’t exist.

    假设一辆车跑在马路上，再把速度无限提高，车子就会消失。我们该如何印证车子的存在？时间就是存在的证明，时间才是唯一的测量单位，时间证明了物质的存在。没有时间，我们就不存在。

  • Professor: All these knowledge, Lucy, I’m not even sure that mankind is ready for it. We’re so driven by power and profit. Given man’s nature. It might bring us only instability and chaos.
    Lucy: Ignorance brings chaos, not knowledge.

    这些所有的知识，我不确定人类有没有资格拥有。我们只想着权力和利益，再加上人性的缺陷。这恐怕只会带来不安和骚动。

    无知才会制造混乱，知识不会。
